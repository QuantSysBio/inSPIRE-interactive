Frequently Asked Questions

Here we try to answer your questions before you asked them. However, if you encounter an issue, it may be easiest to reach out to us directly (see our contact details).

I've started the inSPIRE pipeline. Do I need to leave my laptop running?

If you installed inSPIRE locally and are running inSPIRE-Interactive in local mode, you will need to leave the laptop running. However, if inSPIRE is running on a shared server and you are accessing the server remotely, nothing is running locally. You can safely close your laptop and retrieve results later.

What are the requirements for my user and project names?

The names selected here simply help to divide up the inSPIRE outputs in a sensible folder system. However, we strongly discourage the use of white space in your titles, for example a username of "john.doe" is much better than "john doe".

I closed my browser by accident, how can I get back?

If you had executed the pipeline and just need to retrieve the results go to the start page and select the Retrieve Results workflow. If you had not executed the job just select Run inSPIRE workflow and click the proceed button to get back to where you were working.

My upload seems to be frozen and I don't see any change in the screen.

If you are uploaded from a remote drive (particularly on Windows systems) there's a chance that a network firewall may block the upload. You could try moving the files locally and upload from there. To get more details on the problem you can also right click on the page and select Inspect.
