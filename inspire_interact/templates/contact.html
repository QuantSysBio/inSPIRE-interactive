inSPIRE Help & Support

Our prefered method for standard issues is to open an issue on the inSPIRE Github page. This is a public forum that allows others to learn if you have made a mistake and allows our developers to track problems on our side.

If you are working with sensitive data and are nervous about posting on a public forum or if you are interested in on site support or collaboration you can contact us via email on inspire.team@mpinat.mpg.de
