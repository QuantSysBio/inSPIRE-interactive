Welcome to inSPIRE Interactive !

inSPIRE-interactive is a simple, webserver-based solution for you & your lab to reliably identify greater numbers of peptides from mass spec. experiments via inSPIRE and dive deeper into immunopeptidomics experiments with inSPIRE-Pathogen.

Why inSPIRE-Interactive?

For any mass spectrometry analysis, sensitive and reliable identification is key to maximising potential insights and discovery. The inSPIRE core rescoring provides state-of-the-art identification, while inSPIRE-Pathogen allows you to find pathogen peptides in infected immunopeptidome samples. Further integrations can allow custom searches to be run with MSFragger, binding affinity via NetMHCpan, and quantification via Skyline to build out your analysis ecosystem entirely within inSPIRE-interactive.
